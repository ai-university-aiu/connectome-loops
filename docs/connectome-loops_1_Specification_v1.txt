PURPOSE OF THIS DOCUMENT

This is the Specification phase of a five-phase method called SPARC, which stands for Specification, Pseudocode, Architecture, Refinement, and Completion.

The Specification phase writes down, before any structure or logic is discussed, exactly what the repository must do and the qualities it must have.

This document describes a repository that already exists and is frozen: its code and its behaviour are fixed and unchangeable.

The document therefore describes what was built and why; it does not propose, request, or authorise any change to the code.


WHAT THIS REPOSITORY IS, IN ONE BREATH

connectome-loops is a granularity experiment.

It is the coarse arm of a three-arm study whose single question is: how big should one code-pack be?

A pack is the smallest unit of code that the host platform, PrologAI, can name, load, layer, and test on its own.

This arm holds one answer to the sizing question: one pack per reentrant loop, with the loop's regions kept inside that pack as internal sections rather than as packs of their own.

The deliverable of the repository is not the code.

The deliverable is the finding: a written record, in the files LEDGER.md and COMPARISON.md, of what building the identical brain-circuit anatomy coarsely revealed that PrologAI still cannot express.


THE FIXED EXPERIMENT: ONE VARIABLE, EVERYTHING ELSE HELD CONSTANT

The study varies exactly one thing: the pack boundaries.

Everything else is reused from an earlier, already-built vertical slice named connectome-proto-agi, and is proven to be unchanged.

Held constant, and proven so:

• The behaviour - a cortico-basal-ganglia-thalamo-cortical loop that closes for a chosen number of laps, producing a narrated trace that is byte-identical to the slice's, confirmed by the file-comparison tool diff.

• The data layer - the same twenty-eight structure records written in the Causalontology 3.0.0 data-structure language, byte-identical to the slice's records.

• The dynamics - the same dopamine, cortisol, and synaptic-plasticity arithmetic, reused from the slice's neurochemistry pack without edit.

• The closure mechanism - coordination through a shared coordination store called the Lattice, with zero region-to-region references and no busy-waiting.

• The platform - PrologAI itself, reused read-only and unmodified.

Only the pack boundaries move.

This arm merges the three region packs of the slice into a single loop pack; the three other packs (substrate, dynamics, structure) carry over verbatim.


FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS (WHAT THE REPOSITORY MUST DO)

FR-1  Close the loop.

The repository must tick a reentrant circuit - cortex, then striatum, then thalamus, then back to cortex - for a caller-chosen number of laps, and prove it closed.

The proof is a trace of exactly three-times-the-number-of-laps plus one hops, a strictly increasing token that runs from one to that count, the region sequence cortex followed by that many repetitions of the triple striatum-then-thalamus-then-cortex, and a cortex that re-enters exactly one more time than the number of laps.

FR-2  Keep the regions internal, and uncoupled.

The three regions must live as internal sections of one loop pack, not as separate packs.

Inside that pack the three sections must never call, name, or address one another; they coordinate only by posting numbered phase cues through the Lattice.

FR-3  Reuse the substrate, dynamics, and structure verbatim.

The coordination substrate (neural_lattice), the dynamics (neurochemistry), and the structure records (causal_map) must be the slice's own packs, carried over without behavioural change.

FR-4  Mint and validate the structure.

The repository must mint twenty-eight Causalontology 3.0.0 records describing the circuit's anatomy - one of every kind the cut touches - and validate each against the platform's unmodified conformance engine, including one skipping causal relation and one signed provenance record.

FR-5  Run the honest gates.

The repository must ship runnable checks: a loop runner, a layer checker, a coupling checker, a structure validator, and the in-pack test suites - each exiting zero only when its own condition genuinely holds.

FR-6  Record the findings.

The repository must record, in LEDGER.md, every wall the coarse decomposition hit, and, in COMPARISON.md, the side-by-side measurement against the atomic control arm.


NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS (THE QUALITIES IT MUST HAVE)

NFR-1  Grounding in Causalontology 3.0.0.

Causalontology is the data-structure language every PrologAI part should speak: a process-first foundational ontology in which each structural fact is a content-addressed record whose identity is the hash of its content.

The structure records must be valid against that language's 3.0.0 conformance rules, using the platform's own engine for identity, kinds, semantics, and signing.

The dynamics deliberately stay native arithmetic; a rate constant or a plasticity increment is a number evolving in time, and forcing it into a content-addressed record would be a category error.

NFR-2  The strict layer rule.

Every pack declares a layer number, and a pack may import only packs at a strictly lower layer.

This arm uses three levels: the Lattice at layer zero, the dynamics and the structure at layer one, and the loop at layer two.

The layer checker must pass with zero upward edges.

NFR-3  The closure discipline.

State passes only through the Lattice by stigmergy - a region reads the shared snapshot, acts, and writes the next snapshot - and reactivity is by notification, not by polling.

The count of region-to-region references must be zero, and this must hold even though the three regions now share one file.

NFR-4  The one decomposition rule (this arm's defining invariant).

The coarse rule must be genuinely applied: one pack per reentrant loop, regions internal.

If the arm had produced roughly eleven packs it would have quietly become the atomic arm; it must produce four, with the regions provably internal.

NFR-5  Frozen, unmodified platform.

PrologAI, the companion system Mentova, and the earlier frozen spike must be untouched.

A gap the arm discovers becomes a Ledger entry, never a change to the platform.


OBJECTIVES

• Measure the coarse decomposition rule honestly, on a real circuit, not in the abstract.

• Surface what PrologAI still lacks when a whole circuit becomes one pack.

• Produce numbers - pack count, coupling edges, layer levels, testability - that a later verdict can lay beside the atomic arm and the strata arm.


CONSTRAINTS

• The behaviour, the twenty-eight records, the dynamics, and the closure mechanism are fixed; only pack boundaries may differ from the slice.

• No edit to PrologAI, Mentova, or the frozen spike is permitted.

• Every predicate is pack-qualified with whole words (for example neural_lattice_post_cue), never a terse two-letter prefix.

• Every line of code carries one plain-English comment immediately above it, per the English-Readable-Code rule.

• The full benchmark regression is deferred; only a ten-percent spot-check runs per wave.


ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA (THE GATES IT MUST PASS)

• The loop runner exits zero, and its narrated trace is byte-identical to the slice's (diff clean).

• The coupling checker reports zero region-to-region references and catches an injected cross-region call (so the check is not vacuous).

• The layer checker reports zero upward edges across the three levels.

• The structure validator reports all twenty-eight records valid, the cortisol relation classified as skipping with no gap, and the Ed25519 provenance signature verified.

• Every pack's in-pack test suite passes.

• The mini regression is green: ARC-AGI-1 at forty out of forty and ARC-AGI-2 at twelve out of twelve, a ten-percent spot-check of the full benchmarks (ARC stands for Abstraction and Reasoning Corpus, and AGI stands for Artificial General Intelligence).


REFLECTION ON THE MAIN DESIGN CHOICES

Why coarse at all?

Because the sizing question has no answer in the abstract; it must be paid for in real coupling, real testability, and real layer pressure.

The coarse choice was made precisely so its costs would show up somewhere measurable, and the honest expectation was that its costs would appear inside the pack.

Why keep the closure discipline inside a single file?

Sharing a pack makes direct coupling tempting; a region could simply call its neighbour.

Holding the zero-reference rule even when the regions co-locate is what makes the finding trustworthy: the discipline survived co-location, and a bespoke section-aware checker proves it.

The trade-off this arm accepts.

Fewer, larger packs buy minimal ceremony and almost no cross-pack coupling - two import edges in total - at the price of everything that used to be free at a file boundary.

The internal layer hierarchy becomes invisible to the pack-granular layer rule, a region can no longer be tested without loading the whole loop, and the no-coupling guarantee must be re-implemented as a hand-written section linter.

That single absence - PrologAI has no intra-pack module boundary - is the finding the coarse arm was built to expose, and it is recorded honestly in the Ledger rather than patched into the frozen platform.
