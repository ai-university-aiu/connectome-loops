ABOUT THIS FILE

This is the Architecture phase of SPARC (Specification, Pseudocode, Architecture, Refinement, Completion).

The Architecture phase describes the skeleton: the parts, the layers they sit on, the substrate that carries them, and the interfaces through which they talk.

Acronyms used in this file: Uniform Resource Identifier (URI), JavaScript Object Notation (JSON), Causal Relation Object (CRO), Continuous Integration (CI), Reward-Prediction-Error (RPE).


THE ONE ARCHITECTURAL IDEA

The whole repository is an experiment in where to draw the pack boundary.

A pack is the smallest unit PrologAI can name, load, layer, and test on its own.

This coarse arm draws the boundary around a whole reentrant circuit: one loop is one pack, and the loop's regions are internal sections of that pack.

The contrast arm, connectome-atomic, drew the boundary around each named construct and produced eleven packs; this arm produces four.


THE FOUR PACKS AND THEIR LAYERS

Four packs carve the identical brain-circuit anatomy across three layers.

• neural_lattice - layer 0 - the closure substrate: stigmergy for state plus notification for reactivity, over PrologAI's Lattice. Reused verbatim from the slice.

• neurochemistry - layer 1 - the native dynamics: dopamine RPE, cortisol tone, and the three-factor plasticity rule, as ordinary arithmetic. Reused verbatim from the slice.

• causal_map - layer 1 - all twenty-eight Causalontology 2.0.0 structure records for the anatomy. Reused verbatim from the slice.

• loop - layer 2 - the whole cortico-basal-ganglia-thalamo-cortical circuit, with cortex, striatum, and thalamus as internal region sections.

Three of the four packs are the slice's already-coarse packs, carried over unchanged.

Only the three region packs of the slice were merged into the one loop pack; that single merge is the entire architectural difference between this arm and the slice.


THE STRICT LAYER RULE, APPLIED

Each pack declares a layer number in its manifest, and a pack may import only packs at a strictly lower number.

The three levels here are: the Lattice at zero, the dynamics and the structure at one, and the loop at two.

The loop imports neural_lattice (zero) and neurochemistry (one); both are downward edges, so the rule holds.

The layer checker passes with zero upward edges.

One honest consequence is recorded in the Ledger as LOOPS-1: the slice and the atomic arm placed the three regions at three distinct layers (cortex above striatum above thalamus), but this arm collapses that internal hierarchy into a single layer-two pack, and the pack-granular layer rule can no longer see the ordering the anatomy carries.

THE SUBSTRATE AND THE LATTICE COORDINATION

The Lattice is a shared store of node-facts held at a fixed nexus URI, "locus://localhost/connectome_slice".

Coordination follows two rules.

Stigmergy for state: a region takes the current cue - a snapshot of the whole circulating state - acts on it, and posts the next snapshot.

Notification for reactivity: every write notifies waiters, so a region that awaits a cue blocks on a private queue and is woken by the write, never by polling.

The cue is addressed by a NUMBER, not a name: a region posts phase_0, phase_1, or phase_2, and awaits its own phase.

Because exactly one phase cue exists at any instant, only one region is ever active, which is why the glass-box narration reads as a clean single sequence even though three region threads are alive.

This is the mechanism that lets three sections of one file coordinate without ever referring to one another.


THE INTERFACES BETWEEN PACKS

The loop pack talks to the substrate through the neural_lattice_ predicates: open, reset, post_cue, await_cue, signal_done, await_done, hop, and narrate.

The loop pack talks to the dynamics through the neurochemistry_ predicates: reward, dopamine, plasticity, cortisol_decay.

The driver (bin/run_slice.pl) talks to the loop pack through its three exported ticks - loop_cortex_tick, loop_striatum_tick, loop_thalamus_tick - each run on its own cyclic_actor thread.

The validator (bin/validate_structure.pl) talks to causal_map through causal_map_records, causal_map_signed_assertion, and causal_map_skip_check, and to PrologAI through co_validate_schema, causal_core_validate_semantics, and co_verify_record.

Inside the loop pack the three region sections share no interface at all with one another; their only shared surface is the numbered Lattice cue.


THE STRUCTURE RECORDS (the causal_map pack)

causal_map mints one record of every Causalontology kind the cut touches, and validates each against the platform's unmodified 2.0.0 engine.

The twenty-eight records span these kinds: strata (the neuroendocrine ladder ordinals), continuants (cortex, striatum, thalamus, substantia nigra), occurrents (the events and processes the loop and the hormone realise), a realizable (the striatum's plasticity disposition), ports (the corticostriatal and dopaminergic ports), a bridge, a transmissive conduit (the nigrostriatal dopamine projection), a computational conduit (the corticostriatal projection), a plain CRO (the corticostriatal transform), a skipping CRO (cortisol crossing ten strata with skips true), a token (one cortisol episode), and a signed assertion (Ed25519 provenance over the skipping CRO).

The transmissive-versus-computational decision follows the Perfect Wire Test: a conduit is transmissive when a perfect wire would suffice, and computational when it performs a transformation a wire cannot.

Each record's identity is the hash of its content, computed by causal_core_identify, so identity is byte-exact and any drift would change the hash.


WHERE PROLOGAI IS REUSED, READ-ONLY

Every runnable path locates a local PrologAI checkout through the environment variable PROLOGAI_HOME, defaulting to /home/ccaitwo/PrologAI.

The scripts add the needed PrologAI pack directories to the SWI-Prolog library path at run time; nothing under PrologAI is copied or edited.

The runner adds library(lattice) and library(actors); the layer checker adds library(layer); the validator adds library(causal_core) and the conformance harness; the test runner adds all of these as its packs require.

If PROLOGAI_HOME does not point at a valid checkout, each script prints a clear message and exits with code two rather than guessing.


THE RUNNABLE SURFACE (bin scripts)

• bin/run_slice.sh - assemble the library path and tick the loop; print the narrated trace and the closure verdict.

• bin/check_layers.sh - load PrologAI's own library(layer) and run its directory report and check against this arm's packs.

• bin/check_no_coupling.sh - split the loop module into its three region sections and confirm no section references another (intra-pack analysis, because the regions now share one file).

• bin/validate_structure.sh - validate every structure record, the skip finding, and the signature; write each record to structure/ as JSON.

• bin/run_tests.sh - run every pack's in-pack test suite in its own process.


ARCHITECTURAL REFLECTION

The architecture is deliberately lean: four packs, three layers, two internal import edges.

That leanness is the arm's headline property and its trap in equal measure.

It is the property a system reasoned about one circuit at a time wants - minimal ceremony, minimal cross-pack coupling, a shallow layer stack that projects to roughly twenty-three packs for a full connectome rather than the atomic arm's roughly one hundred forty-three.

It is the trap a system reasoned about one construct at a time falls into - the internal layer hierarchy vanishes, a region cannot be loaded or tested alone, and the no-coupling rule must be enforced by a hand-written section linter rather than by the file system.

The architecture pushes the cost off the pack boundary and inside the pack, which is exactly the trade the experiment set out to measure and exactly what the Ledger records.
