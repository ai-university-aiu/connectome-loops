• Closure - the loop runner exits zero on a proven close: the hop count is three-times-the-laps plus one, the token runs strictly one through that count, the region sequence is cortex then that many repetitions of striatum-thalamus-cortex, and the cortex re-enters exactly one more time than the number of laps.

• Coupling - the coupling checker reports zero region-to-region references across the three internal sections, and it catches an injected cross-region call, so it is not vacuous.

• Layers - the layer checker reports zero upward edges across the three levels (neural_lattice 0, neurochemistry and causal_map 1, loop 2).

• Structure - the validator reports all twenty-eight records valid against Causalontology 2.0.0, the cortisol CRO classified as skipping with an empty skip-gap list, and the Ed25519 provenance signature verified.

• Per-pack tests - every pack's in-pack suite passes.

• Mini regression - GREEN: ARC-AGI-1 at forty out of forty, and ARC-AGI-2 at twelve out of twelve.

The mini regression is a ten-percent spot-check that exercises every tenth task of each benchmark.

It is not the full benchmark run.

It must never be rounded up or restated as four hundred out of four hundred, or as one hundred twenty out of one hundred twenty; the honest figures are forty out of forty and twelve out of twelve, with the full regression deferred by design for this wave.

THE PLATFORM VERSION THIS REPOSITORY WAS BUILT AGAINST

Every runnable path reuses a local PrologAI checkout, unmodified and read-only, located through the environment variable PROLOGAI_HOME (default /home/ccaitwo/PrologAI).

The repository does not copy PrologAI and does not hardcode a specific PrologAI commit hash in its own files; it validates and runs against whatever conforming checkout PROLOGAI_HOME names.

The Causalontology grounding is checked by that checkout's own unmodified 2.0.0 conformance engine and vendored conformance harness, so the structure results are only as strong as the pinned engine the platform supplies.

The reused platform packs are library(lattice), library(actors), library(layer), library(causal_core), library(signing), and the conformance harness under tests/causalontology_conformance.

If PROLOGAI_HOME does not point at a valid checkout, each script stops with a clear message and exit code two rather than producing a misleading pass.


HONEST LIMITS (THE OPEN LEDGER FINDINGS)

The deliverable is the finding, and the findings are open, not resolved.

They are recorded in LEDGER.md under a fresh LOOPS series so they cannot be confused with any other arm's findings.

• LOOPS-1 (severity medium) - a coarse pack collapses the internal layer hierarchy, and the pack-granular layer rule cannot see inside it. The slice and the atomic arm recorded cortex above striatum above thalamus as three layer numbers; this arm records one layer-two pack, and that ordering is simply gone.

• LOOPS-2 (severity medium) - there is no intra-pack module boundary for coupling. The zero-reference guarantee lost its cheap cross-file enforcement and had to be re-implemented as a bespoke section-aware linter; it works, but it is a convention checked by a hand-written script, not a boundary the language provides.

• LOOPS-3 (severity medium) - there is no intra-pack module boundary for testability. A single region cannot be exercised without loading the whole loop pack, because the pack is the smallest unit the platform can load and test.

• LOOPS-4 (severity low) - a second sighting of the slice's finding P5: the layer rule's entry point still needs a hand-written wrapper, and the cost is independent of pack count, biting a four-pack arm exactly as it bit the eleven-pack one.

Three of the four findings are facets of one absence: PrologAI has no intra-pack module boundary, so anything a coarse pack puts inside itself falls below the language's resolution.

What did not become a finding is stated just as plainly in the Ledger: the behaviour held exactly, the twenty-eight records held exactly, and cross-pack coupling nearly vanished to two edges.

The Ledger is deliberately short - four entries, against the atomic arm's seven - and that shortness is itself the finding the coarse rule was expected to produce.


THIS REPOSITORY IS FROZEN EVIDENCE

The code and behaviour of this repository are immutable.

It exists to be measured, not to be extended; a gap it reveals is a Ledger entry, never a change to the code, to PrologAI, to the companion system Mentova, or to the earlier frozen spike.

These six documentation files describe what was built and why; adding them under docs/ touches no code, which preserves the frozen guarantee.

Nothing in these files proposes, requests, or authorises any change to the repository.


WHERE THIS ARM SITS IN THE STUDY

This is one of three arms.

The atomic arm (connectome-atomic) tested one pack per construct and produced eleven packs with high locality but heavy interface coupling.

This loops arm tested one pack per reentrant circuit and produced four packs with minimal coupling but costs pushed inside the pack.

The strata arm (connectome-strata) tested one pack per Causalontology stratum.

The three-arm verdict is drawn once all three arms exist; this file reports only this arm's delivered, frozen state.


COMPLETION REFLECTION

The arm is complete in the only sense that matters for an experiment: it ran, it passed its honest gates, and it produced a clean, comparable measurement.

Its numbers are the leanest of the three arms - four packs, two coupling edges, three layer levels - and its costs are real and named rather than hidden.

The trade-off recorded at completion is the same one the Specification predicted and the Refinement observed: coarse packing buys the "survives many constructs" property by moving the architecture's discipline out of the language and into prose and bespoke scripts, and that migration is the honest limit a reader should carry away.
